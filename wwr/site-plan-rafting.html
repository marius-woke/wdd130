Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my rafting website is to help people experience the thrill and beauty of whitewater rafting.

Audience

My target audience is adventurous people who love nature and outdoor activities. They are mostly young adults between 25 and 40 years old, with a college degree and a moderate income level. They live in urban areas in the US or Canada and are looking for a fun and exciting way to spend their vacation or weekend. They need a reliable and professional rafting company that can provide them with safe and enjoyable rafting trips. They use their mobile devices to access my website, where they can find all the information they need to plan their trip, book their trip online, and share their experience on social media.
